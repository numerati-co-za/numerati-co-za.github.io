Who is behind Numerati?

Sam Isaacson is the principal consultant at Numerati. Sam is a qualified Actuary with more than 15 years of experience. He is an entrepreneur currently performing strategic consulting/advisory work while simultaneously developing an Enterprise Software Product. Prior to this he was a founder of MiWay Insurance where he spent seven years in the role of Chief Actuary. As an EXCO member, Sam gave input on both business & technical matters and was directly responsible for the Actuarial & Business Intelligence functions. Other experience includes running his own Actuarial Consulting firm (Capital Analytics); working as a Consulting Actuary at Deloitte and as an Analyst for Peregrine Structuring. A particular strength is blending his natural understanding & experience in business with his knowledge of Technology and Mathematical Modelling.
